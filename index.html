Overview of Accineur

Accineur derives from the term

  • Accelerate

  • Incubate

  • Entrepreneur

Thus, this program is created to incubate creative business ideas into real business operation and to accelerate young entrepreneurs to build thecapabilities to attract necessary capital and to grow profitably.

Funded by the Interchurch Organization for Development Cooperation (ICCO), and managed by the Cambodia Investor Club Association, The Accineur Program was officially launched on Monday the 31st of August 2015. The launching event took place at the Cambodia-Japan Cooperation Center, and was participated by around 120 agri-business owners and professionals.

What we are looking for

The selected participants must comply to the below conditions

  • General Criteria

    • At least 1 year business operation in Cambodia
    • Locally produced services/products
    • Scalable business

  • Sector/Industry

    • Only services/products/solution related to agriculture business

  • Management

    • At least one skilled manager and a staff
    • Minimum Initial Capital USD 5000
    • Annual Sales > USD 20 000
