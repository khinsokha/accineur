Our Program

The program provides business development and financial supports to any kinds of business ideas that involve innovation and are potential for future growth. Primarily, Accineur puts focus on the agriculture related businesses and in the near future, CiC will try to continue similar project to other business sectors that will allow business owners and entrepreneurs throughout the Cambodia to participate. Not only existing business firm but is also new start-up that will be targeted for the future projects.

Vision

WE ENVISION A PROSPEROUS LOCAL ECONOMY IN WHICH LOCAL ENTER-POSEURS AND INVESTORS ACTIVELY PARTICIPATE IN AND BENEFIT FROM.

Mission

TO BUILD THE CAPABILITIES OF POTENTIAL ENTREPRENEURS TO ATTRACT NECESSARY CAPITAL AND TO GROW PROFITABLY

What we are looking for

The selected participants must comply to the below conditions

  • General Criteria

    • At least 1 year business operation in Cambodia
    • Locally produced services/products
    • Scalable business

  • Sector/Industry

    • Only services/products/solution related to agriculture business

  • Management

    • At least one skilled manager and a staff
    • Minimum Initial Capital USD 5000
    • Annual Sales > USD 20 000
